Research

My research explores how daily experiences, stress, and non-work activities influence employee well-being, recovery, and performance.

Research Overview

I focus on understanding how work and life intersect, particularly how employees recover from stress and how experiences outside of work shape workplace outcomes. My work spans topics such as commuting, affective processes, volunteering, and identity-related experiences in the workplace.

Current Projects

Workplace Experiences & Identity

Investigating how workplace stigma affects sleep and well-being among women of color with ADHD.

Commuting & Recovery

Examining how commuting experiences influence affect, rumination, and safety-related outcomes.

Volunteering & Engagement

Studying how alignment between volunteer motivations and experiences impacts work engagement and recovery.
